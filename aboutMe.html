About Me

Hi there! I'm Alex Roche. I'm a 21 year old london-based freelancer who has a thing for object-oriented programming with Java and Processing, as well as building websites with HTML, CSS and PHP (working on JavaScript, I promise!), with knowledge of mySQL thrown in for good measure. At the same time i'm completing a three year course at Goldsmiths College of London.

With my websites and designs, I aspire to create a clean and interesting site while maintaining an easy-to-read and minimalist page layout, so your clients don't need to spend hours searching for a factoid that should be easily found and even easier to understand.

Away from strict work, I can be found attanding hackdays and programming some weird frankenstein creation. Ignoring the programming environments, however, and i'm an avid computer gamer, consuming everything from Bastion to Battlefront 2 and most things in between. Computer aside, I enjoy getting out and about, cycling, playing the occasional rugby match and spending time with friends, whether it be in a pub or battling dragons and exploring dungeons!

If you've now read through to the end of this and think I can help you, feel free to drop me a line!
