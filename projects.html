    No ce pas un Dictateur

  • Sound produced
  • Proof of concept video

    This one was interesting, mainly because of the time constraints that we had to work in. The result of a crazed frenzy of nearly 30 hours of coding, myself and a small team at the Ravensbourne sponsored music techfest. The video is a proof of concept of a system which, upon receiving video of a speech as input, synthesises both a realtime animation over the speaker's face and processes the sound real-time to produce acompanying music.

    Project ChivalRick

  • Project Proposal

    The result of a session of brainstorming which resulted in a concept for an action tower defense game, similar in concept to defense grid. While we didn't have the manpower to implement the game to our original specifications, we were still able to produce a functioning game. Chivalrick itself is a third person action tower defense game in a medieval setting; you control a player character who can both attack and place towers to defend a castle from waves of enemies.

    HTML 1 compliant browser

    The challenge here was to write a web browser which worked using only Java. To this extent there isn't a particularly good web rendering engine, but all of the standard tools are there such as a favorites list, browsing history, tabbed browsing and even a page source code editor. Too bad it won't see production any time soon or posting for downloading without a lot more elbow grease and polish.
